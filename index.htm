For Students, by Students

We are students who want to help students. We know how busy life can be and we want to make it easier for you to find good food at a good price. We also want to help fellow students who may have lost their jobs due to the pandemic, so we provide them with an opportunity to earn a living through our platform. We hope that you will support our platform and help us grow.

For Canteen Stall Owners

Are you a canteen stall owner? We want to help you grow your business. We provide you with a platform to advertise your products and reach more customers. We also provide you with a platform to manage your orders and payments. This way, you can focus on what you do best: making delicious food. We hope that you will join us and help us build a better future for canteen stalls.
